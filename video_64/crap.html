Tourism Highlights

  • Jaipur (The Pink City): Visit the iconic Hawa Mahal, Amber Fort, and City Palace.
  • Jaisalmer (The Golden City): Explore the sand dunes and enjoy camel safaris.
  • Udaipur (The City of Lakes): Relax at Pichola Lake and marvel at the City Palace.
  • Mount Abu: The only hill station in Rajasthan, famous for Dilwara Temples.
  • Pushkar: Home to the famous Pushkar Camel Fair and the sacred Brahma Temple.
